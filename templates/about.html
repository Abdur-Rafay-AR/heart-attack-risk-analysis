About The Project

Unveiling the patterns in heart health data

Project Overview

This Heart Attack Risk Analysis project leverages advanced data analytics to identify key indicators of heart disease. By visualizing complex medical data, we aim to provide accessible insights into risk factors such as age, cholesterol levels, and heart rate.

The Dataset

The dataset contains medical records of patients, including attributes like:

  • Age & Sex
  • Chest Pain Type (cp)
  • Resting Blood Pressure (trtbps)
  • Cholesterol (chol)
  • Maximum Heart Rate (thalachh)
